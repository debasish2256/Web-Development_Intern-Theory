Welcome

SSNT(Shree Sai Niketan Trust) has now ventured out for a school for special children to reach out to the special needs of visually impaired, hearing impaired, mentally challenged, autism, cerebral palsy and multiple disabilities children

Wood apple – A preparatory school for special children aims at all round development of the kids and making them competent in the worldly affair. Along with basic education it gives special attention to groom the children in their interest area like art, craft, music, single language learning, photography, choreography and many more. Work sheets are prepared taking care of each child’s need and area of development. It is based on 100% one to one teaching learning method.

“Wood Apple as a pre-school”

At such young age, life is so difficult, everyday a struggle! How their life will ever be fullest? Oh, the dreams of the parents have to let go and the things they have to give up.

No… Not any more….

“Wood Apple” as a fruit has great medicinal values for those who consume it. It’s considered as a panacea for various health hazards such as improper digestion, impure blood, respiratory issues, diabetics, kidney ailments, liver health etc. Likewise “Wood Apple as a pre-school” aims to address various issues of aspecially abled child and help them develop and nurture in a safe and simulating environment. We will identify area of interest of the special kids and introduce them to the world of knowledge. If a child is not able to concentrate on his studies, obey his parents or develop his social consciousness, it does not mean that he is bad. It only means that the faculties of brain, which helps in understanding situations, retain black and white images, memories and reproduce, are less capable at that point of time though it has the potential. It needs readjustment to work efficiently. This road to efficiency is set at “Wood Apple” class rooms.
